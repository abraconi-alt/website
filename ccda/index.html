About CCDA

A National Movement for Community Wholeness

The Christian Community Development Association (CCDA) is a network of Christians committed to seeing people and communities experience God's shalom — restoration that is spiritual, emotional, physical, economic, and social.

"We inspire, train, and connect Christians who seek to bear witness to the Kingdom of God by reclaiming and restoring under-resourced communities." CCDA Mission Statement
🏙️

Based in Chicago, Illinois

Headquartered on the West Side of Chicago, CCDA works nationwide across under-resourced communities in the United States.

🤝

National Network of Practitioners

CCDA connects thousands of community development leaders — pastors, organisers, educators, and advocates — through shared training, events, and peer connection.

📖

Rooted in the CCD Philosophy

Built on the 8 components of Christian Community Development, including reconciliation, relocation, redistribution, and leadership development.

🌱

Mentoring at the Heart

Leadership development — through mentoring, coaching, and shared learning — is central to CCDA's mission to sustain and empower grassroots leaders.

The CCDA Mentoring Programme

Developing Leaders Who Transform Communities

CCDA uses the Stellar Mentoring platform to connect experienced community development leaders with emerging practitioners — building the next generation of people who will serve and restore under-resourced communities across America.

🌱

Stellar helped CCDA connect seasoned community development practitioners with emerging leaders — passing on decades of lived experience across neighborhoods and cities.
